About us

We are a home and lifestyle brand with an edgy New York point of view. Our products are designed in Brooklyn and responsibly manufactured by the world’s best artisans.

Our approach

Design does not exist in isolation; it is a reflection of the larger society. As society changes, so should design. The best designers iterate their designs rapidly to keep pace with society’s demands.

This is our belief at Le Monde Zeya... Good design redefines itself, rapidly.

Our designers create objects that are both beautiful and useful. We believe form does not follow function and function must not be held responsible for a drab form.

We strive to create organic designs for our clients- designs that will fulfil all their wants and at the same time become a part of culture, not just commerce.
